LET'S GO DIVING

Scuba Diving Course

If you have already tried scuba diving, and you liked it, and you want to know more, be more independent under the water and dive in the places for certified divers, then the Open Water Diver diving course in Phuket is where you need to start. The course is divided into several parts: theory, practice, and of course, diving itself. It's simple: you go through all these parts of training in parallel: theory and practice in the pool or shallow water, and then real dives in the sea. During the training, you will learn everything that a diver should know: how the equipment works and how to use it and take care of it; where and when you can dive and how to plan your dives, how to swim under the water. You will learn so much that every day will bring you more and more vivid impressions!

Certificates

PADI Open Water Course

A course for beginners with an international certificate. As part of the course, students are introduced to diving equipment, the basics of scuba diving, and various techniques. Upon successful completion of the course, a PADI Open Water Diver certificate is issued, which is recognized worldwide. To get it, you need to attend theoretical classes, make 1–2 dives in the pool with an instructor and 4 dives in open water (at sea), correctly complete the necessary actions and fill out the test exam.

  • ✔ Quantity of dives: 4+pool
  • ✔ Approximate Duration: 3–4 days
  • ✔ Prices starting from:
    115.00 USD (Adult)
    80.00 USD (Child)
  • ✔ Minimum Age: 10 years old

Experienced instructors: Learn the basics of scuba diving from a friendly and highly qualified team of trainers.

Practice: The course includes theoretical lessons and the implementation of mandatory exercises in the pool, plus 3–4 dives in the sea.

Diving from childhood: The course is available for kids from 10 y.o. For students aged 10 to 12 years, the maximum diving depth is 12m.

PADI Advanced Open Water Diver (AOWD)

The PADI Advanced Open Water Diver course consists of 5 dives, two of which are mandatory – a deep sea dive (no deeper than 30m) and underwater navigation. The remaining 3 dives are selected at the discretion of the student. It can be: AWARE fish identification, ship diving, towing dives, current diving, mastery of buoyancy, night dive, multilevel dives and computer dives, search and recovery of sunken objects, underwater naturalist, underwater photography, wreck diving.

  • ✔ Quantity of dives: 5
  • ✔ Approximate Duration: 2 days
  • ✔ Prices starting from:
    300.00 USD (Adult)
    200.00 USD (Child)
  • ✔ Minimum Age: 10 years old

Experienced instructors: Learn to be a confident scuba diver from a friendly and highly qualified team of trainers.

Practice: The course includes 5 dives on different depth with implementation of exercises and exam passing.

Diving from childhood: the course is available for kids from 10 y.o. For students aged 10 to 12 years, the maximum diving depth is 12m.

PADI Divemaster Course

The PADI Divemaster course is the first professional level. The course consists of an extensive theoretical part, and also includes assistance to the instructor in the pool and in open water. To join the course, you need to have a PADI AOWD or equivalent certificate . Also you should have a PADI Rescue Diver certificate or equivalent, be at least 18 years old and have a good physical shape. The duration of this course depends on your previous experience as by the end of the course, you need to have at least 60 registered dives in open water.

  • ✔ Quantity of dives: no less that 60
  • ✔ Approximate Duration: 15 days
  • ✔ Prices starting from:
    1000.00 USD (Adult)
    800.00 USD (Child)
  • ✔ Minimum Age: 18 years old

Experienced instructors: learn to be a proficient scuba diver from a friendly and highly qualified team of trainers.

Vast opportunities: this course is your first step to becoming a professional diver. You'll become a PADI member with all benefits.

Privileges: subscribe to the Undersea Journal, receive newsletters from the PADI head office and discounts on purchases.
